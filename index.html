My Story

A journey of starting over, learning the system, and growing with purpose.

From Zimbabwe

I came to the United States to pursue the American Dream. At first, I focused mostly on making money before I truly understood what it takes to become successful in a new country.

📚

Finding Direction

I originally wanted Civil Engineering because I was strong in math and physics. I chose cybersecurity for the wrong reasons at first, and learning how computers communicate was difficult until I started becoming serious about the process.

The Turning Point
